# Welcome to BioFAIR

[Website](https://biofair.uk/) | [LinkedIn]([https://x.com/BioFAIRUK](https://www.linkedin.com/company/biofair-uk/)) | [Contact](mailto:info@biofair.uk)

BioFAIR is a federated digital research infrastructure for UK life sciences, driving a step-change in Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Re-usable (**FAIR**) research data management and reproducible data analytics for the UK life sciences. BioFAIR is funded by UKRI.

---

## The BioFAIR Commons

Our ecosystem brings together three interconnected pillars designed to streamline data-driven bioscience:

* **Data Commons:** Supporting end-to-end FAIR research data management from collection to long-term community archive deposition. 
* **Method Commons:** Providing national platforms for trusted, reproducible workflows, analytical pipelines, and tools.
* **People Commons:** A pooled network of BioFAIR Fellows, professional data stewards, and Research Software Engineers (RSEs) assisting researchers nationwide.

---

## Featured Initiatives & Projects

* **Pathfinder Projects:** Funding innovative community-driven solutions tackling critical FAIR bottlenecks across UK life science domains.
* **BioFAIR Fellowships:** Bringing together a diverse cohort of individuals from across the UK life sciences ecosystem to drive sustainable cultural change around FAIR adoption.
